Hatteras Inlet

Hatteras Inlet is located 27 kilometers to the north of Ocracoke Inlet and 19 kilometers west of Cape Hatteras along southeast facing Hatteras Island. The inlet, which marks the boundary between Hyde and Dare Counties, opened during a major hurricane in September 1846, the same time as Oregon Inlet to the north. Old Hatteras Inlet, located 16 kilometers to the west of the current inlet, was opened as early as 1585 and remained open until the late 1700s.
