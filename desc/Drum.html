Drum Inlet

Drum Inlet is an artificial inlet located on undeveloped Core Banks, approximately 40 kilometers northeast of Cape Lookout. The inlet connects Core Sound with the Atlantic Ocean, and thereby provides a navigation corridor for the town of Atlantic on the mainland.​​


The current Drum Inlet was opened in 1971, several months after Old Drum Inlet closed. The new inlet, located approximately 3.5 kilometers southwest of the old inlet, provided an expedient route to the offshore fishing grounds.
